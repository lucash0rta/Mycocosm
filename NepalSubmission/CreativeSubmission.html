Creative Response Critical Reflection Documentation: Research Material

To exist is to change

Through research, reflection and many conversations with our group surrounding colonialism, beauracracy, intention and documentary ethics we collaborated on attempting to capture perspectives during this current moment where a gradual economic shift is happening in Kawasoti. With the vulture restaurant leading a pathway to a new model of eco-tourism.

This process began with discussions surrounding scepticism towards the vulture restaurant and learning centre and was finalised in this attempt to collect perspectives and to display them through a medium that strived toward minimal imprinting of personal biases or the aestheticisation of the different cultures, stories or people.

The website is intentionally kept offline and a copy is in the hands of Nagma and myself. With this being personal stories about the area the intention was for this project to be locally stored by somebody, however showcasing this in a gallery setting permanently or hosting it online does not feel right at this point in time with the limited number of opinions we were able to record from people in the area. It would be completely biased and could serve as a voice to other people via assumption;; The project would need further time, effort and considerations to be exhibited in the area of Kawasoti or anywhere.
