Progress With Evidence

This week, I focused on refining my mockup models for a fake gallery space, locking in the final forms of the sculptures and taking detailed measurements. I also explored AI tools, particularly Marigold depth estimation, to experiment with extracting depth from images. These experiments aimed to create digital works that evoke the textures of moulds or rock carvings, blending organic and digital aesthetics.

In addition, I reconfigured my Raspberry Pi, installed Linux, and disassembled an old pair of Bluetooth headphones to test new speakers by directly connecting cables to the soundboard. After successful testing, I decided to purchase a soldering kit to integrate this into my final project.
