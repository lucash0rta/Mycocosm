Shumon Basar - EndCore and LoreCore

additional notes in [[LORECORE - Shumon Basar]] & [[The Dawn of Endcore - Shumon Basar]]

Today I have read through Endcore and LoreCore by Shumon Basar Accesible here - https://flash---art.com/article/endcore/ https://zine.zora.co/the-laws-of-lorecore-shumon-basar

Through these essays Shumon is capturing a feeling, a vibe, shifts in timelines and the general feeling of the internet.

LoreCore is the MCS (Main Charachter Syndrome) many experience as a part of our curated lifestyles on the net. Aswell as stories we tell ourselves and others to romanticise our lives including identity politics.

I am more interested in EndCore. The idea is best summarised in this quote -

"After the end of the end of History. Endcore is the feeling we're in the endgame now: the endgame of climate grief, of technocapitalism, of consensus-based empirical truth, of decency, even. Endcore is the sense of an ending that many people on Earth currently feel, but also the sense that the end never actually arrives. We are Permaending. Cue more Lorecore."

When He mentions Permaending Shumon is referencing the current state of this anthropocentric world. A state of perma-crisis

"an extended period of instability and insecurity, esp one resulting from a series of catastrophic events" - Collins English Dictionary

This cataclysmic forever ending is edging us as we never quite reach a climax. It's lead to this unique cacophonous feeling. A mixture of Awe, Horror, Mindlessness, Brainrot, Loss, Spectacle and Sublime.
