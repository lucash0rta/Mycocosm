Progress with Evidence

Audio Development

I dedicated significant time to refining the audio in my scenes, working iteratively between Ableton and the video piece. This involved adding foley and atmospheric sounds that complemented the visuals. For the first portion of the video, I even incorporated MIDI instruments.

Physical Object Refinement

I've been applying several coats of paint to achieve a cleaner finish on the finalized object, focusing on eliminating layer lines to achieve a smooth white finish on the doll. Simultaneously, I've been designing and testing the final box design.

This process presented several challenges:

  1. Insufficient space allocation for screen cables within the box
  2. Limited access to plywood at the makerspace (1 sheet per day allowance)
  3. Difficulty sourcing 90-degree cable adapters from Jaycar

I ultimately modified an HDMI cable using various tools - a delicate process that, despite some close calls, proved successful.

The spray paint sourcing journey was equally challenging:

  • Bunnings: Undergoing renovations
  • Art supply store: Sold out of white spray paint
  • Builder's supply store: Mistakenly provided temporary spray and mark paint
  • Finally succeeded at Eckersleys in the city
