Research

This week I conducted research into esp32 code bases for displaying images and running motors, how to setup the wiring and research into vertical motor setups as the motor rotates 180 degrees I need to convert this into a vertical setup with my push puppet re design I am thinking of

I also did some research into the alternative use cases of the esp32's. Due to the onboard bluetooth and wifi functionalities the esp32 can support local hosting of websites, I find this interesting as an idea for the final piece to be able to incorporate qr code interactions for links to either further text, an essay, or the interactive websites I had built earlier in the semester.

I also conducted some preliminary research into how or if it would be feasible to use cheap subwoofers from aliexpress.

This method is a bit more complicated than I initially thought as it needs additional voltage flowing into the speaker where you need a driver, the amplitude effects the sound output volume and resonance and therefore it complicates things and a driver is necessary. Due to this being somewhat more complex than I had anticipated makes me think I should not pursue this method.
