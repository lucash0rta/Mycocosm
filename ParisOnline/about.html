About Us

Parisonline is a digital archive of multidisciplinary art projects stretching from Stockholm, Paris and beyond. A cultural platform for telling unpredicted stories through fictionalized journalism.

Founded in 2021 by Tova Olsson and Julia Eklund.

For collaborations and business inquiries: parisonline.mag@gmail.com
