Thanas

“Un petit morceau d'ambiance de la chambre où tout est cassé (A little ambiante piece from the room where everything is broken).”

A film on broken instruments featuring the French musician Louise, known as Thanas. After a summer of touring she is back home with her best friend Névé. Shot in their home in the south of France.
