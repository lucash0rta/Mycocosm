Thanas

A film on broken instruments featuring the French musician Louise, known as Thanas. After a summer of touring she is back home with her best friend Neve, and composes "un petit morceau d'ambiance de la chambre où tout est cassé (ENG: A little ambiante piece from the room where everything is broken)". Shot in their home in the south of France.

Music:
We go way back by AE (a Thanas project)
